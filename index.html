Key Impact Areas:

NRG Radio

Collaboration: Give & Grow Initiative

Collaborated for the 'Give & Grow' initiative, providing media coverage and platform support to reach a wider youth audience. This collaboration focuses on amplifying youth voices and fostering talent development through media engagement.

Ministry of Shipping & Maritime

Colaboration: Blue Economy & Conservation

Collaboration on the 'Blue Economy' awareness campaign, focusing on sustainable ocean resources and conservation of marine life in coastal regions. Together, we work on educating communities about the importance of our maritime heritage and sustainable practices.

Ministry of Health

Collaboration: Mental Health Advocacy

Joint advocacy for mental health awareness, destigmatizing mental illness, and providing resources for psychological support in correctional facilities. This collaboration aims to bridge the gap in healthcare access for vulnerable populations.

DEFCO

Collaboration: Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

Supported through Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) funding, enabling the distribution of essential supplies and support for community development projects. Their contribution has been pivotal in our prison rehabilitation and community outreach programs.

County Government of Taita Taveta

Collaboration: Cultural & Tourism Ambassador

Official collaborated as Cultural & Tourism Ambassador, promoting the rich heritage, tourism sites, and investment opportunities of Taita Taveta County. This role involves representing the county at national forums and showcasing its diverse culture.

Mental Health Advocacy

Focus: Destigmatization & Support

We work to destigmatize mental illness, particularly within correctional facilities. Our programs provide counseling services, support groups, and mental health education to inmates and staff, fostering an environment of healing and understanding.

Climate Action

Focus: Conservation & Sustainability

Championing environmental stewardship through reforestation projects, such as our initiatives at Manyani Maximum Prison. We advocate for sustainable community practices and align our efforts with SDG 13 (Climate Action) and SDG 15 (Life on Land).

Human Rights

Focus: Dignity & Justice

Advocating for the fundamental rights of incarcerated persons. We focus on ensuring humane treatment, access to legal resources, and rehabilitation opportunities that respect the dignity of every individual, regardless of their past.

Empowerment

Focus: Youth & Women

Nurturing the next generation of leaders through mentorship and education. We provide platforms for youth and women to develop skills, achieve economic independence, and become active change-makers in their communities.

Cultural Heritage

Focus: Preservation & Promotion

Celebrating the rich diversity of Kenyan culture, with a special focus on Taita Taveta traditions. We use global platforms to showcase our heritage, arts, and tourism potential, fostering pride and cultural continuity.

Peace Building

Focus: Harmony & Conflict Resolution

Promoting conflict resolution and harmony within communities. Our initiatives aim to bridge divides, foster dialogue, and build safer, more cohesive societies for everyone.
